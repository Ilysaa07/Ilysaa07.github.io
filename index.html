About Us

Miracle Mates is a clothing brand based in Bandung, Indonesia, founded in 2014 by Fadli Maulana Ibrahim.
The founder has a great interest in things that related to Skateboard and streetwear fashion.
Why we called it “Miracle Mates”? as we know Miracle have meaning a surprising and welcome event that is
not explicable by natural or scientific laws and is therefore considered to be the work of a divine agency
and Mates because our brand come out with a support from many friends thats why we collaborate the name with “Mates”.
